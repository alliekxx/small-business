About

Our story

The equestrian club and the racetrack are places where you can not only engage in equestrian sports, but also spend time with benefits for your health and mental balance. Equestrian sports are not only an exciting activity, but also an opportunity to take care of horses, participate in competitions and communicate with people who share your passion for equestrian sports.

One of the main directions in the work of equestrian clubs and racetracks is hippotherapy. This is a method of treatment and rehabilitation using horse riding. Hippotherapy is used to treat many diseases such as cerebral palsy, cerebral palsy, disorders of the musculoskeletal system, autism spectrum disorders and others. Horseback riding helps strengthen muscles, improve coordination and balance, and stimulates the cardiovascular and respiratory systems.

In general, equestrian sports and hippotherapy are important areas in the work of equestrian clubs and racetracks. Here you can find not only activities for children and teenagers, but also for adults who want to spend time with benefits for their health and mental balance. Equestrian clubs and racetracks host various events such as cultural events, exhibitions, horse care workshops and more. Equestrian sports and hippotherapy can be available for people with different levels of physical fitness and health. Equestrian sports can be a great opportunity for people who want to start playing sports but don't know which sport to choose. Hippotherapy, in turn, can be useful for people who need rehabilitation and treatment, as well as for those who want to strengthen their health and develop coordination of movements.
